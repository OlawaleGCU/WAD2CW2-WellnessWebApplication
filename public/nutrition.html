Nutrition Explained

Food nutrition studies how food affects the body and how it uses its nutrients to maintain health, grow, and function properly. It involves studying the nutrients present in food, the way they are digested by the body, and how food affects health.

Food contains nutrients essential for our bodies to function properly: carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water. As well as providing energy, building and repairing tissues, regulating body processes, and supporting immunity, nutrients play a specific role in the body. A balanced diet should include foods from all the major nutrient groups in appropriate amounts to maintain good health and prevent chronic diseases. The best way to achieve this is by consuming adequate amounts of whole grains, lean protein sources, healthy fats, fruits, vegetables, and low-fat dairy products.

In addition to nutrition, food safety and food preparation are also important factors for preventing foodborne illnesses and maintaining food quality.
